Studio Headphone

Design

The Ripple design is

inspired by different

musical notes.

It is an attempt to create an elegant form

which does not restrict but rather elevates

the functionality of the headphones

The three part headband is light

and comfortable.

The assembly is connected to move

seamlessly as the headband is

extended and contracted.

The weave mesh equally

distributes the weight

Listen to every detail
